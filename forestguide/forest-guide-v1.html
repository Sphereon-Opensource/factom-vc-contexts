Terminology

issueDate

Date at which Forest Guide (GTF) was issued.

expirationDate

Date at which Forest Guide (GTF) expires and is no longer valid.

countryOfOrigin

The origin country where the Forest Guide (GFT) is issued.

regionalForestAuthority

Regional Forest Authority which issued Forest Guide (GTF).

resourceOrigin

The type of allowance for the origin of the resource(s) in a given GTF listing.

resourceNumber

The number (e.g. Concession Number) corresponding to the resourceOrigin.

fullHolderName

The full name of the holder of the resource origin entity.

resolutionNumber

The resolution identification number for the resource corresponding to its origin or concession value.

department

The department responsible for the Forest Guide (GTF) issuance.

province

The province for or in which the Forest Guide (GTF) was issued.

district

The district for or in which the Forest Guide (GTF) was issued.

forestManagementPlan

The type code of the Forest Guide (GTF) plan.

gtfOwner

The owning entity of the GTF.

gtfRUCNumber

The RUC number corresponding to the owning entity of the GTF.

gtfAddress

The full address corresponding to the owning entity of the GTF.

gtfDepartment

The department corresponding to the owning entity of the GTF.

gtfProvince

The province in which the owning entity of the GTF operates.

gtfDistrict

The district in which the owning entity of the GTF operates.

gtfBillNumber

The bill number that is covered with the GTF.

gtfProofOfPurchaseOrSaleType

The type of the proof of purchase or bill of sale of the GTF.

addressee

The addressee (recipient) information of the GTF and its privileges.

addresseeRUCNumber

The RUC number of the addressee (recipient) of the GTF.

addresseeAddress

The mailing address of the addressee (recipient) of the GTF.

addresseeDepartment

The department of the addressee (recipient) of the GTF.

addresseeProvince

The province of the addressee (recipient) of the GTF.

addresseeDistrict

The district of the addressee (recipient) of the GTF.

carrierNumber

The carrier (delivery agent) delegated by the GTF.

carrierTransportationType

The type of transportation used by the carrier (delivery agent) delegated by the GTF.

carrierVehicle

The vehicle type used by the carrier (delivery agent) delegated by the GTF.

carrierLicensePlate

The license plate of the GTF carrier's vehicle.

carrierDriver

The name information of the delivery driver (carrier) of the GTF.

carrierIdentityDocumentType

The identity document type information of the delivery driver of the GTF.

carrierIdentityDocumentNumber

The identity document number of the driver (carrier) of the GTF.

carrierDriversLicenseNumber

The driver's license number (if applicable) of the driver (carrier) of the GTF.

productDetailsLogListNumber

The log listing number for a specific Product Listing covered in a GTF.

productDetailsRelatedGTFNumber

The related GTF listing number for a specific Product Listing covered in a GTF.

productDetailsScientificName

The genus name of the origin tree for a specific Product Listing covered in a GTF.

productDetailsScientificName

The species name of the origin tree for a specific Product Listing covered in a GFT.

productDetailsCommonName

The common (non-scientific) name of the origin tree for a specific Product Listing covered in a GTF.

productDetailsType

The type for a specific Product Listing covered in a GTF (e.g.: logs, boards).

packagingMethodDescription

A description of the packing method corresponding to the quantity units for a specific Product Listing covered in a GTF.

packagingMethodQuantity

The quantity packed for a specifically packed Product Listing covered in a GTF.

productDetailsUnitOfMeasurement

The units used for a specifically packed Product Listing covered in a GTF.

productDetailsTotal

The total quantity value for a specifically packed Product Listing covered in a GTF.

logNumber

The identifying Log Number for a specifically packed Product Listing covered in a GTF, if applicable.
